eFootball 2025 Introduces Real-Time Manager Mode

Konami’s biggest leap in football simulation — from the dugout to the sidelines.

Konami Revamps the eFootball Experience

Konami has officially announced eFootball 2025, marking a major milestone for the series with the introduction of the Real-Time Manager Mode. This new feature gives players the power to oversee their club's strategies live, making tactical adjustments during matches without pausing gameplay.

The update is designed to bring football fans closer to the sidelines than ever before. Players will be able to read in-game momentum, react to opponents' formations, and switch playstyles dynamically — all through an immersive interface inspired by real coaching systems.

Beyond the new mode, eFootball 2025 introduces enhanced player animations and a refined ball physics engine. Konami has also collaborated with real clubs to record authentic sideline communications, aiming to replicate the adrenaline and pressure of real-world football management.

Early access players who tested the closed beta reported smoother online play and a more responsive control system. The addition of new stadium atmospheres, weather effects, and realistic crowd chants elevates the immersion even further.

eFootball 2025 launches globally in November 2025 on PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. Mobile users will also receive an optimized version later this year, ensuring the football fever continues across every platform.
