Nintendo Direct Unveils New Zelda & Mario Titles

A legendary showcase brings new adventures to the Switch lineup.

A Celebration of Iconic Franchises

Nintendo’s latest Direct 2025 broadcast delivered what fans had been waiting for — the return of two of its most beloved series. The gaming giant revealed The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Eternity and Super Mario Odyssey 2, both scheduled to launch later this year on the Nintendo Switch 2.

Echoes of Eternity follows a mysterious narrative branching across parallel worlds, with Link navigating between realms to restore balance. The gameplay trailer showcased vast floating islands, dynamic sword combat, and a hauntingly beautiful score by returning composer Koji Kondo.

Meanwhile, Super Mario Odyssey 2 looks to expand upon the sandbox freedom that made its predecessor a global phenomenon. Mario and Cappy return with new mechanics such as multi-capture forms and cooperative play, letting two players explore kingdoms together for the first time.

Fans also got glimpses of several surprise reveals — including remastered classics like Metroid Prime 2: Echoes HD and a new Donkey Kong Country reboot. The showcase highlighted Nintendo’s continued mastery of merging nostalgia with innovation, celebrating its heritage while looking boldly to the future.

Nintendo closed the presentation by confirming a major summer 2025 event in Kyoto to celebrate the 40th anniversary of Super Mario. Rumors suggest a hardware tie-in or limited-edition console may accompany these blockbuster launches, making 2025 a milestone year for fans around the world.
